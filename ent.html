ENT Care & Treatment

About ENT

ENT (Ear, Nose, and Throat) conditions affect hearing, breathing, and speaking. These problems are usually caused by infections, allergies, pollution, or poor hygiene. Common issues include ear pain, blocked nose, sore throat, sinus infections, and allergies.

Rest & Recovery

Take proper rest to help your body fight infections. Avoid dust, smoke, and cold exposure. Staying in a clean and comfortable environment helps in faster recovery.

Hydration

Drink plenty of warm fluids like water, soups, and herbal teas. This helps keep the throat moist and reduces irritation.

Healthy Diet

Eat warm and nutritious food such as fruits and vegetables. Avoid cold drinks, oily foods, and spicy items that may worsen symptoms.

Home Remedies

Steam inhalation helps clear nasal blockage. Salt water gargling relieves sore throat. Honey and ginger can soothe throat irritation.

Medication

Medicines like antihistamines, nasal sprays, and pain relievers can help. Avoid antibiotics unless prescribed by a doctor.

Hygiene & Care

Maintain hygiene by washing hands regularly and avoiding touching your face. Do not share personal items and avoid inserting objects into ears.

When to See a Doctor

Consult a doctor if symptoms last more than a few days or if you have severe ear pain, hearing loss, breathing difficulty, or nose bleeding.

Medicines Required

Normal

Cetirizine (for allergy)
Paracetamol (pain relief)
Steam inhalation (supportive)

High

Antihistamines (Levocetirizine)
Nasal sprays (Oxymetazoline)
Mild antibiotics (doctor prescribed)

Severe

Strong antibiotics
Steroid nasal sprays
Surgical treatment (if chronic sinus/ear issue)
